Nelson v0.2.1
What's new:
===========
* No more spamming nelson-news: Nelson grabs all the posts in a neat one and done message to post into the channel

* Music is here: Nelson can stream audio from a YouTube link, run "/help" or "@nelson help" to learn more

* Butler: A couple of commands added to give Nelson some more utility

* Nelson will alert server to emojis being updated, channels created, or channels deleted

* Better /ask handling
